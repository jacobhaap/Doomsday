U.S. Doomsday Map

This map is intended to display information that may be useful in a United States doomsday scenario. I am not a professional by any means, but believe that locations of nuclear plants (shown in orange), locations that may be the target of nukes/bombings (shown in red), and areas where civil unrest is likely (yellow highlighted areas) is information necessary to include.

Disclaimer: Such a situation taking place within the United States of America is highly unlikely. This map is to serve for purely theoretical purposes, and not to be used as an actual guide should such a situation arise.
